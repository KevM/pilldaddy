Designed to Lighten the Caregiver's Mental Load

Routine Dose Planner combines routine dose checklists with professional care-continuity logging, ensuring you never lose track of what was given and why.

Color-Coded Routines

Group medications into distinct routines based on the time of day (e.g. morning, lunchtime, bedtime). Custom pill colors provide clear visual guidance for stress-free assembly.

The "Why?" Reasoning Journal

Caregivers swap meds or adjust dosages during stressful weeks. Routine Dose Planner requires capturing the clinical reasoning note for every change, ensuring a traceable, successor-linked history.

Reminders & Live Activities

Get reliable local notification reminders when a routine is due. A persistent Live Activity sits on the lock screen, acting as a gentle "pester" cue until the doses are checked off.

One-Way Apple Health Sync

Easily log vital metrics like Blood Pressure, Pulse, Oxygen Saturation, Weight, and Water Intake. The app synchronizes these readings locally to Apple Health, leaving them safe in your secure medical database.

Strict Personal Privacy

No user profiling, no account creation, and no advertising trackers. Your data is stored strictly locally using SwiftData and synchronized only via your private Apple iCloud container.
